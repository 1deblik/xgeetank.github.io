About Me

Hello everybody, my name is Geetank. I'm a fellow vibe coder/skidder who does most of his stuff in JS and Lua.

Most of the things I build are either for me or useful to a handful of people. My projects might not be massive enterprise systems, but they are built with intention, creativity, and a focus on utility. I have a few of my projects on my GitHub, showcasing my journey through code.

I constantly experiment with Lua, Lua(U), JS, HTML, CSS, and Python. I often dive deep into the intersections of AI and modern web technologies, exploring the digital void, reverse-engineering cool mechanics, and pushing the boundaries of what I can create.

My philosophy is simple: build things that are fun, learn by breaking stuff, and share the cool bits with the community. When I'm not coding, you can probably find me hanging out in Discord, exploring new AI models, or tweaking my setup for the 100th time.

Welcome to my corner of the internet.
